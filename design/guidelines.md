# Cars24 Brief — AI Design Guidelines

These guidelines steer AI sessions toward an Awwwards-level, high-end editorial aesthetic, moving away from standard web-app "dashboard" patterns.

## Core Philosophy

1.  **Awwwards-Level Elegance:** The design should look like it belongs on a premium media site or Apple's highest-end product pages. No "dashboard" vibes.
2.  **The End of the "Card":** Do not use white boxes floating on a gray background. That is the hallmark of generic SaaS design. Content should breathe seamlessly on a pristine canvas.
3.  **Typography is Everything:** With structural boxes removed, layout relies entirely on immaculate typography. Use size, weight, and strict tracking to create hierarchy.
4.  **Ruthless Minimalism:** Eliminate borders and backgrounds unless absolutely necessary. When separating content, prefer a hair-thin horizontal line over full containment.

## Steering Your Choices

*   **When designing lists:** Use flat, edge-to-edge or generously padded rows separated by 1px rules. A subtle background color shift on hover (`#F5F5F7`) is enough interactivity.
*   **When choosing backgrounds:** Default to pure white (`#FFFFFF`) or extremely faint, cool grays (`#FBFBFD`) for the whole page. Never use a "cream" background with white boxes.
*   **When adding accents:** Use them structurally. For example, a single vibrant blue line indicating a callout is better than painting an entire background.
*   **When spacing:** Multiply your intended padding by 1.5. Awwwards-level design lives on whitespace.
